Please set your keyboard shortcut:
  • Open a new tab.
  • Type chrome://extensions in the address bar and hit enter.
  • Click the “Keyboard shortcuts” link on the bottom-right.
  • Under the header Ferro, locate the item “Activate the extension.”
  • Click the text box to the right.
  • Press a key combination. Alt-Shift-F is recommended.
  • After pressing the key combination, you should see that combination displayed in the text box.
  • At this time, you can also set the other available Ferro shortcuts, for example Alt-Shift-P for toggling whether the current tab is pinned.
Thank you!
